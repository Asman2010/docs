## Viewing Teacher Comments

Access your recent teacher feedback through the Comments section.

### Comment History

- View your last 10 teacher comments
- Each comment includes:
  - Teacher's name
  - Date of comment
  - Comment content
  - Associated points (if any)

<img src="/images/student-comments.png" alt="Teacher Comments Interface" />

<Note>
  The system displays the 10 most recent comments from your teachers. Older comments may be archived.
</Note>

### Accessing Comments

1. Click the "Check Comments" button from your dashboard
2. View the chronological list of teacher feedback
3. Use this feedback to track your progress and areas for improvement
