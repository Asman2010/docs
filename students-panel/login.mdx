## Login Process

Students can access their dashboard through a secure login process.

### Steps to Login

1. Select your class from the dropdown menu
2. Enter your administrator-provided Name
3. Enter your password
4. Click "Login" to access your dashboard

<Note>
  Your login credentials are provided by the administrator. If you're having trouble logging in, please contact your administrator.
</Note>

<img src="/images/student-login.png" alt="Student Login Interface" />

### Important Points

- Names are case-sensitive
- Make sure to select the correct class
- Keep your credentials secure and don't share them
