## Dashboard Overview

The dashboard is your central hub for viewing points and accessing various features.

### Points Display

Your current points are prominently displayed in a card format, showing:
- Total points earned
- Recent point changes
- Point history

<img src="/images/student-dashboard.png" alt="Student Dashboard" />

### Quick Actions

From your dashboard, you can:
- Check available rewards
- View teacher comments
- Track your progress

<Tip>
  Keep an eye on your points regularly to track your progress and available rewards!
</Tip>
