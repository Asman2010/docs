## Generating Reports

The report generation feature provides comprehensive insights into student performance.

### Requirements

- Student must be selected
- Minimum of 10 teacher feedback entries required

### Accessing Reports

You can access reports in two ways:
1. Using the "Generate Report" button on the main page
2. Through the "Generate Report" option in the sidebar

<Warning>
  Attempting to generate a report without selecting a student or meeting the minimum feedback requirement will result in an error.
</Warning>
