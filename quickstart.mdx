## Before You Begin

Ensure you have:
- Valid institutional email
- Account credentials (provided by your administrator)
- Supported web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)

## First-Time Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Your Portal">
    Choose your designated portal:
    - **Teachers**: Visit [teacher.pristinepathway.com](https://teacher.pristinepathway.com)
    - **Students**: Visit [student.pristinepathway.com](https://student.pristinepathway.com)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Initial Login">
    1. Click "Login" in the top right corner
    2. Enter your institutional email
    3. Use the temporary password provided
    4. Set up your new password
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Profile">
    Update your profile information:
    - Verify contact details
    - Set communication preferences
    - Upload profile photo (optional)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Quick Actions Guide

### For Teachers

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Managing Points">
    1. Select class from dropdown
    2. Choose student(s)
    3. Click increase/decrease points
    4. Select point value
    5. Add reason (required)
    6. Submit
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Generating Reports">
    1. Navigate to Reports section
    2. Select reporting period
    3. Choose report type
    4. Select student(s)
    5. Generate and download
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### For Students

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Viewing Points">
    1. Login to student portal
    2. Check dashboard for current balance
    3. View transaction history
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Redeeming Rewards">
    1. Go to Rewards section
    2. Browse available items
    3. Select desired reward
    4. Confirm redemption
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting Tips

<Note>
Common issues and quick fixes:
- **Can't Login**: Check caps lock and verify email
- **Points Not Updated**: Refresh page after 5 minutes
- **Report Generation Failed**: Ensure minimum feedback requirement is met
</Note>
